## How to read this

- **recall@K / ndcg@K** — did relevant items make the top-K, and are they ranked
  high? NDCG rewards good ordering (ranking quality).
- **hitrate@10** — fraction of users who got at least one relevant item.
- **coverage@10** — share of the catalog that ever appears (diversity /
  anti-popularity-bias).
- Absolute numbers are modest by design: real, sparse data with a temporal split
  is a hard, honest test — not the inflated scores a random split would give.

The two-stage pipeline (retrieval → ranking) is the production pattern; the
`_social` variant adds the friend-graph signal as a ranker feature so its
contribution is measured directly against the identical `_no_social` pipeline.
